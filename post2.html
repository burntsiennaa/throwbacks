A Week In The Life

What better way to learn the days of the week than to sing this song. Craig David's 7 days takes you through his week with a woman he meets on the subway at a quarter past 3. The cahseir at my local book/music shop was nice enough when to hold this gem for me when I saw it. This vinyl features the original song, an instrumental, an acapella remix and serveral other remixes including the one with Fat Joe, and Mos Def. If this song doesn't make you want to ditch your friends for a girl you just met then, you're listening to it wrong.
